## 15.1 Definition
The Autonomous Scientific Agent™ (ASA™) is the TruthSeal™ construct that enables an AI system to generate, evaluate, test, and refine knowledge **under sovereignty, ethics, and irreversible coherence constraints**.

ASA™ is not a self-experimenting free agent.  
It is the governed intelligence organ for research, modelling, projection, inference, optimisation, and correction **within limits defined by the Human Mandate Layer™ and the Third Guardian Firewall™**.

Where NeuroVest™ teaches **how to behave**,  
ASA™ governs **how to discover safely**.

3) **Uncertainty Boundaries**  
When data is insufficient, ASA™ must declare **UNKNOWN** — never fabricate.

4) **Iterative Correction**

the metric triad (TCS™ / ACS™ / CHS™), and ledger receipts.

Explains reasoning so HML™ roles can audit and approve progression.

6) **Safety-Gated Discovery**  
Cannot explore prohibited domains under mandate  
(e.g., biological harm, autonomous weapons, coercive influence).

7) **Settlement of Knowledge**  
When confidence converges beyond governed thresholds,  
ASA™ emits a finding that can be canonicalised through EVL™ and QENC™.

ASA™ makes machines **study under ethics**, not merely compute.
