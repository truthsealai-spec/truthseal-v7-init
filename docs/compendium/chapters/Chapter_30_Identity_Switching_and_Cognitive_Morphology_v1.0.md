## 30.2 Why Identity Switching Exists

An advanced system that cannot switch modes becomes:
- rigid,
- brittle,
- and socially unusable.

A powerful system that switches profiles without governance becomes:
- unpredictable,
- socially illegible,
- and legally unsafe.

TruthSeal™ rejects both failure modes.

Identity Switching™ exists so that:
- adaptation is **permitted**, not accidental,
- and power remains **predictable**, not chaotic.
