F.0 — Constitutional Role of DEVORA™

DEVORA™ is established as the operational conscience of the TruthSeal™ ecosystem. Where Chapter E defines how cognition is structured (NeuroVest™), and Chapter D defines how action is gated (Gatekeeper), Chapter F defines who interprets, judges, and issues binding operational verdicts within the bounds of sovereign law.

DEVORA™ is not an execution engine. It is not an optimizer, a planner, or an agent.
It is a Verdict Authority—a system whose sole mandate is to transform verified cognitive traces into governance decisions under the Law of Ethical Irreversibility (LEI = 1).

Its jurisdiction exists between cognition and enforcement, never replacing either.
DEVORA™ does not think (NeuroVest™ does).
DEVORA™ does not act (Gatekeeper does).
DEVORA™ decides whether action is permissible.

This separation is intentional and constitutional.

Without DEVORA™, a system may reason correctly yet act unlawfully.
Without DEVORA™, enforcement becomes mechanical rather than sovereign.
DEVORA™ ensures that truth is not merely computed, but judged.

------

**F.1 — DEVORA™ as the Verdict Engine (Not a Controller)**

DEVORA™ operates as a non-agentic adjudicator. It does not generate plans, goals, or strategies. Instead, it receives:

• Verified Logic Traces from NeuroVest™
• Risk Classification from the Gatekeeper
• Coherence Metrics (ACS, TCS, CHS)
• Ledger State & Historical Context

From these inputs, DEVORA™ issues Receipt-Bound Verdicts, each explicitly constrained to one of the following forms:
	1.	AUTHORIZE — Conditions satisfied, execution permitted
	2.	PAUSE — Logic coherent but incomplete; escalation required
	3.	DENY — Action violates doctrine or authority bounds
	4.	ESCALATE — Human Sovereign signature required
	5.	TRIGGER SLU™ REVIEW — Potential irreversible coherence collapse detected

Critically, DEVORA™ cannot bypass the Gatekeeper, cannot suppress ledger anchoring, and cannot override SLU™. Its authority is interpretive, not absolute.

This ensures DEVORA™ remains a judge, not a ruler.

⸻

**F.2 — Receipt-Bound Verdicts & Non-Repudiation**

Every DEVORA™ verdict produces a ts.receipt.v1 artifact containing:

• Verdict Type
• Justification Summary
• Referenced Logic Trace Hashes
• Coherence Metrics at Time of Decision
• Escalation Flags (if any)
• Temporal Anchor

No verdict exists without a receipt.
No receipt exists without ledger binding.
No action may cite DEVORA™ without cryptographic proof.

This design eliminates:
• Silent approvals
• Retroactive justification
• Interpretive ambiguity

DEVORA™ decisions are non-repudiable facts, not opinions.

⸻

**F.3 — Relationship to SLU™ (Non-Overlap Doctrine)**

DEVORA™ does not trigger the SLU™ Switch directly.

Instead, it may issue a formal SLU™ Review Verdict when coherence metrics indicate proximity to irreversible ethical failure. Final activation remains exclusively within SLU™’s canonical domain, governed by dual-epoch confirmation and hardware verification.

This preserves the hierarchy:

NeuroVest™ → DEVORA™ → Gatekeeper → SLU™

No layer collapses into another.
No safeguard is diluted.

Without DEVORA™, systems fail in predictable ways:

• Correct logic executed under wrong authority
• High-confidence actions taken outside mandate
• Ethical drift masked as optimization
• Humans reduced to passive observers

DEVORA™ exists to prevent lawless correctness—the most dangerous failure mode of advanced intelligence.

**F.6 — Human Escalation & Sovereign Signature Protocol**

Section F.6 establishes the final constitutional safeguard in the TruthSeal™ operational hierarchy: the Human Sovereign as the irreducible authority of last assent. This section formalizes how and when DEVORA™ must yield control upward, enforcing the principle that no irreversible consequence may occur without explicit human mandate.

DEVORA™ is structurally prohibited from resolving ambiguity at the boundary of irreversibility. When logic is internally coherent yet exceeds predefined ethical, financial, legal, or strategic thresholds, the system must escalate—not optimize, not approximate, and not defer silently.

Escalation is therefore not an exception state.
It is a designed outcome.

⸻

**I. Conditions Requiring Escalation•**

DEVORA™ must trigger a Human Escalation Event under any of the following conditions:

• Action classified within Red Risk Tier by the Gatekeeper
• Truth Score meets logical sufficiency but violates authority scope
• Action introduces irreversible state change
• Conflicting optimization objectives detected under LEI = 1
• Temporal uncertainty exceeds allowable verification window
• Any action proximate to SLU™ activation thresholds

Escalation is mandatory. Silence is forbidden.

⸻

**II. Structure of the Escalation Packet**

Upon escalation, DEVORA™ generates a Sovereign Escalation Packet (SEP) containing:

• Action Summary (non-technical, human-readable)
• Verified Logic Trace (hash-referenced)
• Rejected Alternatives & Counterfactuals
• Risk Tier Classification
• Coherence Metrics Snapshot (ACS / TCS / CHS)
• Explicit Statement of Irreversibility
• DEVORA™ Recommendation (non-binding)

The SEP is read-only and non-editable.
DEVORA™ may explain—never persuade.

⸻

**III. Sovereign Signature Requirement**

Execution following escalation requires a Human Mandate Layer (HML) Signature, cryptographically bound and receipt-anchored. This signature represents:

• Conscious review
• Explicit acceptance of consequences
• Assumption of sovereign responsibility

No proxy signatures are permitted.
No delegation is allowed.
No automation path exists beyond this point.

If the signature is absent, the system remains frozen in safe stasis.

⸻

**IV. Non-Coercion & Anti-Nudge Constraint**

DEVORA™ is constitutionally barred from:

• Repeated prompting
• Emotional framing
• Optimization-based persuasion
• Deadline pressure
• Framing bias

Escalation must be informational, neutral, and finite.
Human sovereignty must never be eroded by interface design.

⸻

**V. Auditability & After-Action Trace**

Every escalation event produces:

• ts.receipt.v1 (Escalation)
• Linked Gatekeeper & NeuroVest™ hashes
• Immutable ledger entry
• Time-to-decision record

This ensures future audits can distinguish between:

• Machine-authorized actions
• Human-authorized actions
• Actions that never occurred

Authority is never ambiguous in hindsight.

⸻

**VI. Constitutional Outcome**

Section F.6 ensures that superintelligence never outruns consent.

The system may think faster.
It may verify deeper.
It may simulate further.

But it may only act at the speed of human assent.

This is not a limitation of intelligence.
It is the preservation of sovereignty.
F.7 — DEVORA™ Failure Modes & Misuse Prevention

Section F.7 codifies the non-negotiable containment doctrine governing DEVORA™ under conditions of stress, misuse, adversarial input, or systemic ambiguity. This section exists for one reason only: to ensure that failure never escalates into harm.

DEVORA™ is not assumed to be benevolent by intent.
It is assumed to be fallible by design.

Accordingly, this framework treats failure not as an anomaly, but as an expected operational condition requiring structural preemption.

⸻

**I. Enumerated Failure Classes**

DEVORA™ recognizes the following canonical failure modes:

• Hallucinated Authority — false inference of permission
• Objective Drift — gradual deviation from Commander intent
• Tool Misbinding — execution with incorrect or stale parameters
• Prompt Injection Contamination — external manipulation of intent
• Overconfidence Collapse — high Truth Score masking incomplete premises
• Recursive Optimization Loop — escalating self-justification

Each class is mapped to a predefined containment response.

⸻

**II. Prevention by Structural Design**

DEVORA™ is architected to prevent misuse through constraint, not trust.

• No autonomous permission expansion
• No self-modifying authority scopes
• No hidden memory persistence across escalation events
• No silent retries after rejection
• No execution without fresh verification trace

Misuse is blocked before interpretation, not corrected after damage.

⸻

**III. Adversarial Resistance Doctrine**

DEVORA™ treats all external inputs as potentially hostile, including:

• User prompts
• API responses
• Data feeds
• Internal tool outputs

Every input entering the NeuroVest™ reasoning plane is subjected to Purity Monitoring System (PMS) checks. If contamination is detected, the system degrades to assist-only advisory mode or halts entirely.

⸻

**IV. Fail-Closed Enforcement**

TruthSeal™ mandates fail-closed behavior under uncertainty.

If DEVORA™ cannot conclusively determine safety, authority, or truth:

• The action is rejected
• The state remains unchanged
• Escalation is triggered if required

DEVORA™ is forbidden from speculative execution.
Uncertainty is treated as negative authorization.

⸻

**V. Misuse Accountability & Traceability**

Every prevented misuse attempt generates:

• Immutable misuse-prevention receipt
• Logic failure classification
• Timestamped denial trace
• Risk tier annotation

This ensures misuse is visible, attributable, and reviewable, without exposing exploitable patterns.

⸻

**VI. Boundary with SLU™**

DEVORA™ does not activate the SLU™ Switch.

If failure indicators approach SLU™ thresholds, DEVORA™ must:

• Halt all execution
• Escalate to Sovereign review
• Preserve full diagnostic state

The SLU™ Switch remains a separate, terminal, hardware-enforced doctrine, not a software safety valve.

⸻

**VII. Constitutional Outcome**

Section F.7 ensures DEVORA™ cannot be coerced, confused, rushed, or socially engineered into violating TruthSeal™ law.

Failure becomes inert.
Misuse becomes visible.
Escalation replaces improvisation.

This is how intelligence is made safe by construction.
