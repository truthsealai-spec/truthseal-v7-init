## 20.2 Safety as a Constitutional Outcome

TruthSeal™ defines safety as **constitutional**, not optional.

Safety is not:
• a checklist,
• a public claim,
• or a compensatory score.

Safety must satisfy four immutable properties:

1) Safety must exist **before scale**  
2) Safety must be **non-compensatory**  
3) Safety must be **receipt-provable**  
4) Safety must be **stable across time**

A system that violates any property loses legitimacy under TruthSeal™ doctrine.
