# TruthSeal™ — Public Product Boundary (v1.0)

Purpose:
Define what TruthSeal™ IS and IS NOT in all public materials.

TruthSeal™ IS:
• A system that certifies provenance, integrity, and auditability of AI & AGI outputs.
• A provider of cryptographic receipts and verification records.
• A standard for coherent, accountable AI & AGI behaviour.

TruthSeal™ IS NOT:
• A description of internal enforcement mechanisms.
• A disclosure of veto, halt, custody, or audit logic.
• A disclosure of internal decision paths or guardians.
• A disclosure of Doctorantura-only components.

Public materials MUST:
• Follow Public_Term_Exposure_Rules_v1.0.md
• Avoid Doctorantura-only terminology and mechanics
• Describe outcomes, not internal control structures

Status: CANON — Public Boundary
